About My Crochet Journey 🧶



Hey! Welcome to Crochetbysp
We’re Prerna and Shavi — best friends, creators, and the people behind this little crochet corner of the internet. What started as a fun hobby and a way to relax quickly turned into something we genuinely love doing together. After spending hours experimenting with yarn, patterns, and ideas, we decided to create Crochetbysp to share our handmade pieces with others. At Crochetbysp, we make a variety of crochet items like bags, keychains, tops, bookmarks, gloves, toys, and cute hair accessories. Every piece is handmade by us with patience, creativity, and lots of trial and error (and sometimes tangled yarn ). We love turning simple yarn into something unique that people can actually use and enjoy. For us, crochet isn’t just about making products — it’s about creating something personal, cozy, and a little different from mass-produced things. Each item we make carries a bit of our time, effort, and creativity.

People choose Crochet by SP because we keep it real—unique designs, careful quality, and custom options made just for you. We care about details, listen to what you want, and make pieces that are cute, durable, and totally one-of-a-kind. When you shop with us, you’re not just buying crochet… you’re supporting a small dream, slow handmade art, and something made especially with you in mind 🧶✨

Thank you for supporting handmade art and helping a small dream grow 🌸
